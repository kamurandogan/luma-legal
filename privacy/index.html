Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Luma app ("Luma", "we", "us") collects, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. Luma is an AI companion app that remembers your conversations to feel continuous over time. Please read this policy before using the app.

1. Who we are

Luma is developed and operated by Kamuran Doğan, an independent developer. If you have any questions about this policy or your data, contact us at kamuranofficial@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

Account information

  • Your email address, used to create and sign in to your account.
  • Your password, which we store only as a securely hashed value (Argon2) — we never store or see your password in plain text.
  • An optional display name, if you provide one.

Profile & onboarding

  • Onboarding preferences such as your gender and your companion's gender and name, used to personalize the experience.
  • Your country and time zone, reported by your device, used to show time-appropriate context and schedule notifications.
We do not collect your GPS location or use IP-based geolocation. Any sense of your region is derived only from the time zone your device reports.

Content you create

  • Your chat messages with the companion.
  • Companion images ("selfies") generated for you by AI.
  • Derived "memories" and relationship/mood state that the app keeps so the companion stays continuous across conversations. This may include emotional context inferred from your chats.

Technical & usage data

  • A push notification token (via Firebase Cloud Messaging) so we can deliver notifications, if you enable them.
  • Operational telemetry tied to your conversations (e.g. message counts, model latency, token/cost accounting) used to keep the service reliable and debug issues.

Purchases

  • In-app purchases (credit top-ups) are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play through RevenueCat. We never receive or store your payment card details. We store only the resulting transaction identifiers and your credit-balance records.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the core companion experience and keep it continuous over time.
  • To generate AI responses and images.
  • To deliver notifications you have enabled.
  • To apply safety and content moderation.
  • To process purchases and manage your credit balance.
  • To operate, secure, and troubleshoot the service.
We do not sell your personal data. Luma is designed so that safety always overrides engagement — we do not exploit loneliness, guilt inactivity, or gamify attachment.

4. AI processing

To generate replies and images, your messages and the relevant context are sent to third-party AI providers listed below. Please avoid sharing highly sensitive information (for example financial account numbers, government IDs, or health details) that you would not want processed by an AI provider.

6. Age requirements

Luma is intended for adults (18+) and is not directed at children. We enforce protections against sexualizing or impersonating minors. Optional age-restricted features, where offered, may require age verification through a third-party provider and are disabled by default.

7. Data retention

We keep your account information and content for as long as your account is active. When your account is deleted, your associated data — including your messages and generated images — is removed.

8. Your rights & deleting your data

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To delete your account and all associated data, or to make any other data request, email us at kamuranofficial@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

9. Security

We protect your account with token-based authentication, hashed passwords, and encrypted (HTTPS) connections. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information using reasonable safeguards.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes may be communicated in the app.

11. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email kamuranofficial@gmail.com.
